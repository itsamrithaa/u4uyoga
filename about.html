About U4U Yoga

U4U stands for "Yoga For You." It's a philosophy built on the belief that yoga should meet you exactly where you are, with compassion, understanding, and unwavering support. We are dedicated to creating a safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment where healing and self-discovery can flourish.

Meet Safara

Safara is a certified yoga instructor with specialized training in trauma-informed practices and yoga for digestive health. Her personal journey through healing has ignited a profound passion for holding space for others.

She believes in the power of gentle movement, mindful breathing, and somatic awareness to calm the nervous system, foster a loving connection with the body, and create pathways to resilience. Her qualifications and compassionate approach make her a trusted guide for anyone seeking a more peaceful and embodied life.
