Our Mission & Vision

At U4U Yoga, we are guided by a deep commitment to creating wellness spaces that are not only inclusive and accessible but also fundamentally just and healing for all.

Our Trauma-Informed Principles

Our mission is to provide yoga-based wellness that is deeply rooted in trauma-informed care. We honor the following principles in every class and interaction:

  • Safety: Creating a secure and predictable environment, both physically and emotionally.
  • Choice: Offering options and encouraging you to make choices that feel right for your body.
  • Collaboration: Working together to create a shared experience of healing and support.
  • Trustworthiness: Maintaining clear boundaries and consistency to build a foundation of trust.
  • Empowerment: Recognizing your inherent strengths and supporting your capacity for healing.

Focus on Gut-Brain Healing

We place a special focus on the profound link between our gut and our brain. Through mindful movement, targeted breathwork, and somatic practices, we help soothe the nervous system and support digestive health. We believe that a calm gut contributes to a calm mind, and our practices are designed to nurture this vital connection.

Our Long-Term Vision

Our vision extends beyond the yoga mat. We aim to foster a community where wellness is a tool for social and healing justice. We are committed to building partnerships, expanding our accessibility initiatives, and advocating for a wellness culture that is equitable, inclusive, and honors the lived experiences of all individuals.
