# U4U Yoga | LISC Massachusetts Digital Growth Project

This repository contains the technical implementation for **U4U Yoga**, developed as part of the **LISC Massachusetts Digital Growth Accelerator**. 

### 🏆 Project Context
I built this platform as a lead technical contributor during a competitive digital acceleration program, where it was awarded **1st Place** for its excellence in digital strategy and technical execution (Action Plans). The goal was to provide a high-impact, professional digital presence for a local small business to scale their community healing initiatives.

### My Work
* **End-to-End Development:** Architected a custom "compassionate minimalist" interface tailored to trauma-informed healing practices.
* **Technical Strategy:** Translated business mission into a functional, SEO-optimized web platform to drive community engagement.
* **UX/UI Design:** Developed an accessible, mobile-first experience prioritizing ease of use and nervous system regulation.
